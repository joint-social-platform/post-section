Blocking

Manage Blocking

Restricted list

When you add someone's profile to your Restricted list, they won't see posts on Momentom that you share only to Friends. They may still see things you share to Public or on a mutual friend's profile, and posts their profile is tagged in. Momentom doesn't notify your friends when you add them to your Restricted list

Block users

Once you block someone, that person can no longer see things you post on your timeline, tag you, invite you to events or groups, start a conversation with you, or add you as a friend. Note: Does not include apps, games or groups you both participate in.

Block messages

If you block someone's profile on Momentom, they won't be able to contact you in Messenger either. Unless you block someone's Momentom profile and any others they may create, they may be able to post on your timeline, tag you, and comment on your posts or comments
